About me

I’m a postdoctoral researcher working at the intersection of human–computer interaction, extended reality (XR), and generative AI. My recent work focuses on context-aware 3D content generation for augmented reality, empathic human–AI interaction using physiological signals, and multimodal AI pipelines that bridge language, vision, and immersive environments.

With a background in Management Information Technology (Business Intelligence) and Computer Science, I combine strong data engineering skills—databases, ETL, BI dashboards, and cloud platforms—with HCI research and experimental XR systems. I enjoy building end-to-end pipelines: from data and models to real-time, interactive prototypes that are evaluated with users.

My long-term goal is to design AI-powered interactive systems that are not only intelligent, but also trustworthy, empathic, and accessible in everyday AR/VR experiences.
